What we offer

  • Crowdsourced labor

    We handle the annotators for you, so no need to look for them on your own.

  • Categorization

    We provide annotation in the form of categorization, with more options coming soon!

  • Automated moderation

    Inside Katze, we have automated moderation in the form of reactive and proactive strategies, so you won't have to worry about bad actors.

  • AI powered analysis

    Katze automatically provides a preliminary analysis using classifier models, sentiment analysis, and string stemmers to provide annotators the best experience.

  • Crypto-based payments

    At Katze, we love Dogecoin and crypto. You get to receive and pay in crypto.

  • Simple Dashboard

    To ensure the best user experience, the dashboard is intentionally simple but powerful, allowing you to navigate easily.
